Process:

    Step 1 "Asset extraction":

  • The initial step involved using Glidors UE Viewer to extract the requisite models and textures from the game "Batman: Arkham Asylum" .

    • Step 2 "Blockout":

  • After acquiring the assets, the blockout phase commenced, which entailed referencing an image from the game to create the level.

    • Step 3 "Decorating" :

  • This phase involved the manual placement of the extracted game assets, including windows, cables, and ironwork.

    • Step 4 "Lighting" :

  • Although potentially tedious, lighting is a straightforward stage that requires the strategic placement of lights in the scene and baking them to optimize performance.

    • Step 5 "Detailing" :

  • The final stage entails adding detailed touches, such as wall cracks, bullet holes, and water puddles, through the application of decals. This step is relatively easy and creates a peaceful, relaxing atmosphere.
