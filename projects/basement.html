Process:

    Step 1 "Asset creation":

  • Initially, the asset and props creation were carried out using Blender . Subsequently, textures were generated in Photoshop by implementing simple techniques such as image downsizing and modification according to the requirements. The UV unwrapping process was achieved through projecting from view (UV > Project from view) or Cube Projection (UV > Cube Projection), and the UV was further refined to achieve the desired outcome.

    • Step 2 "Blockout":

  • The blockout stage involves patience and is relatively straightforward . Real-time csg is utilized to create a new model, which is inverted (for interior levels only), followed by the placement of brushes to construct the room. Placeholder assets are then placed to determine their placement, with different materials used for the props and levels during this phase.

    • Step 3 "Decorating" :

  • The decoration stage is enjoyable and peaceful. real-time csg brushes are replaced by the props.

    • Step 4 "Lighting" :

  • The lighting stage can be tedious, but it is uncomplicated. It involves placing lights in the scene and baking them for optimal performance.

    • Step 5 "Detailing" :

  • The final stage involves adding details, such as wall cracks, bullet holes, and puddles of water, through the application of decals, which is an easy and relaxing process.
